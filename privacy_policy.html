Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-26

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Hulshouser Dental Lead Recovery ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information submitted through our messaging and lead follow-up services.

Information We Collect

  • name
  • phone number
  • email address
  • requested service information
  • preferred appointment timing
  • other booking-related information voluntarily provided by the user

How We Use Information

  • respond to patient inquiries
  • help dental practices follow up on missed calls or inbound inquiries
  • support appointment booking workflows
  • improve message routing and operational performance

Information Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared only with the participating dental practice and service providers required to operate the messaging workflow.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information submitted through our systems. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as reasonably necessary for business, legal, or operational purposes.

Messaging Consent

By providing a phone number and opting in to receive messages, users agree to receive booking-related and customer care text messages associated with their inquiry. Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. Users may reply STOP to opt out and HELP for assistance when supported.
