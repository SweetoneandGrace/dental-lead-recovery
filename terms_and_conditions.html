Program Description

Hulshouser Dental Lead Recovery provides messaging workflows designed to help dental practices respond to inbound patient inquiries and booking-related requests.

Message Types

Users may receive customer care and booking-related text messages after contacting a participating dental practice or submitting an inquiry through an approved opt-in flow.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies depending on the user’s interaction and booking needs.

Costs

Message and data rates may apply according to the user’s mobile carrier plan.

Opt-Out

Users may opt out of future text messages by replying STOP when supported.

Help

Users may request help by replying HELP when supported.

Consent

By opting in, users agree to receive recurring customer care and booking-related messages related to their inquiry.

Participating Practices

Messages may be sent on behalf of participating dental practices using the Hulshouser Dental Lead Recovery platform.
